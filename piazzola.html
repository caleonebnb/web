The Piazzola

Spacious and tranquil pitches for tents, campers and caravans, set within the grounds of the Ca' Leone agriturismo farm. The perfect choice for those who love to travel light and sleep close to nature.

Each pitch has access to on-site facilities including shared shower and toilet blocks, a washing-up area, and the outdoor BBQ area. Electric hook-ups are available on request for campervans and caravans.

Wake up to the sounds of the farm, wander through the vegetable garden, pick fruit from the orchard, and enjoy the genuine pace of rural life in the Bolognese hills.

An ideal base for cyclists and hikers — the farm sits at the edge of the Apennine foothills, with trails of all levels accessible directly from the site. Bologna city centre is just 25 minutes by car.
